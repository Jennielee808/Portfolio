Old 'Jen'Gaming

E-commerce site

Old 'Jen' Gaming is an E-commerce site meant for vintage gaming consoles. This site has no backend and this is accomplished by using Commerce.js(an eCommerce API). You can add items to your cart, view your cart and then make a payment.

I used Stripe as the payment method seen above.
